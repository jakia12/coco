How To Buy

Step 1:

Download and set up a compatible wallet, such as Phantom Wallet.

Step 2:

Buy Solana (SOL) on a cryptocurrency exchange and transfer it to your wallet.

Step 3:

Visit Raydium in your Phantom, Wallet browser and connect your wallet.

Step 4:

Copy and paste the contract address into Raydium or Phantom Wallet token search, and select coco pops "$COCO" as your desired token and swap SOL for the amount you want to buy.
